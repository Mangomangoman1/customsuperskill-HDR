FAQ

Common questions

Is my device safe in the mail?

Yes. We send detailed packing instructions and our prepaid labels include $500 insurance. We have shipped hundreds of devices with zero losses.

What if you cannot fix it?

No fix, no fee. We will tell you honestly if it is not repairable and ship it back for $12. No diagnostic charge, no hidden fees.

How long does it take?

Most repairs ship back within 2–4 business days of arrival. Screen replacements often ship next day. You get a tracking number both ways.

Do you repair devices from outside Idaho?

Our focus is Idaho, but we do accept mail-ins from neighboring states (MT, WY, UT, OR, WA). Same process, same warranty.
